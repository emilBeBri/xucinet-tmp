Burkhardt_GovernmentAgency {xUCINET}R Documentation

Advice among employees in a government agency collected by Burkhardt and Brass.

Description

Network data capturing who communicated with whom about work matters at a government agency at five points in tie. The data were collected by Burkhardt and Brass on 75 employees. See $ProjectInfo for more information.
